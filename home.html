BENEFITS OF YOGA

Improves Flexibility and Posture:

Regular yoga practice helps stretch and lengthen muscles, improving overall flexibility. Better flexibility reduces the risk of injuries and enhances posture, preventing back and neck pain caused by prolonged sitting or poor alignment.

Reduces Stress and Anxiety:

Yoga incorporates deep breathing and meditation, which activate the body's relaxation response. This helps lower stress hormones, calm the mind, and promote mental well-being, making it an effective tool for managing anxiety and daily stress.

Boosts Strength and Balance:

Many yoga poses require the engagement of core and stabilizing muscles, improving strength and balance. Over time, this enhances physical endurance and coordination, benefiting athletes and individuals of all fitness levels.

Enhances Overall Health:

Yoga has been linked to improved heart health, better digestion, and enhanced immune function. It promotes mindful living, encourages healthier habits, and can help regulate blood pressure and blood sugar levels.
